Math Explorers Grants Program

Supporting college students to create vibrant mathematical communities in schools across the country.

How It Works

Our grants program empowers college students to start and run Math Circles in their communities. Each $500 grant provides the resources and support needed to create a lasting mathematical community.

1

Apply

College students apply for a grant to start a Math Circle at a school of their choice.

2

Train

Selected students receive training and resources to run an engaging Math Circle.

3

Impact

Students create a vibrant mathematical community that inspires curiosity and deep thinking.

Grant Requirements

To ensure the success of each Math Circle, we require:

  • College student applicants must demonstrate mathematical interest and leadership potential
  • Schools must provide a suitable space and support for the Math Circle
  • Math Circles must meet regularly (weekly or bi-weekly)
  • Students must document and share their experiences
