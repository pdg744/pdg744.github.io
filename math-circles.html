What are Math Circles?

Collaborative Learning

Math Circles are collaborative learning environments where participants work together to explore mathematical ideas through engaging problems.

Natural Discovery

Originating in Russia, this approach emphasizes natural discovery and deep thinking over memorization.

Engaging Problems

We present carefully chosen problems that spark curiosity and discussion, leading to natural mathematical discoveries.

How They Work

In our Math Circles, we present carefully chosen problems that spark curiosity and discussion. As participants work together to solve these problems, they naturally discover mathematical concepts and develop their problem-solving skills.

Small Groups

Work in intimate groups where everyone's voice is heard and valued.

Guided Exploration

Our facilitators guide the discussion while encouraging independent thinking.

Shared Discovery

Learn from others' perspectives and build on each other's ideas.

What to Expect

"Each session is designed to be engaging and accessible, regardless of your mathematical background. You'll work in small groups, share ideas, and discover that mathematical thinking is both natural and enjoyable."
