Math Circles

Experience the joy of mathematical discovery together with your child.

What are Math Circles?

Math Circles originated in Russia as a way to engage students in genuine mathematical thinking. The concept is simple: present an interesting problem, and let the learning unfold naturally through discussion and exploration.

How It Works

In our Math Circles, parents participate alongside their children. You'll experience firsthand how a well-chosen problem can engage both 8-year-olds and adults, sparking genuine mathematical curiosity and deep thinking.

What to Expect
