What are Math Circles?

Community Learning

Math Circles are vibrant learning communities where participants explore mathematical ideas through collaborative problem-solving and discussion.

Volunteer-Led

Led by passionate volunteers from the community, Math Circles create spaces where mathematical exploration is a shared journey.

Engaging Problems

We present carefully chosen problems that spark curiosity and discussion, leading to natural mathematical discoveries.

How They Work

In our Math Circles, we create spaces where mathematical exploration is a natural part of community life. As participants work together to solve engaging problems, they develop deep mathematical thinking and build lasting connections.

Community Building
