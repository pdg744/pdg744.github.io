Essential Resources

Activity Library

San Francisco Math Circle offers an extensive collection of engaging, hands-on activities designed with "low floors and high ceilings" - accessible to beginners but rich enough for deep exploration.

Math Circle Network

The Math Circle Network provides comprehensive information about the history, philosophy, and impact of Math Circles in the United States. Their resources are invaluable for understanding the broader context of Math Circle education.

Circle in a Box

This essential guide by Sam Vandervelde provides practical advice on organizing, supporting, and sustaining a Math Circle. It includes sample documents, warm-ups, and presentation materials.
